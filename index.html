Ransomeware Attack On Cognizant

April 19

IT service provider compant Cognizant said that it has faced a ransomeware attack on saturday which cause disruptions to its client. The company has already released a statement about that attack on their official website saying "Cognizant can confirm that a security incident invoilving our internel systems and causing service disruptions for some of our clients, is the result of a MAZE ransomeware attack." The company also added that the ransomeware is communicating with their clients."Our internel security teams, supplemented by leading cyber defence firms,are actively taking steps to contain this incident. Cognizant has also engazed with the appropiate law enforcement authorities. We are ongoing communication with our clients and have provided them with Indicators of Compromise(IOC) and other technical information of a defensive nature."
